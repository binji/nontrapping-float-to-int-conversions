Result Types¶

\[\begin{split}\begin{array}{llclll@{\qquad\qquad}l} \def\mathdef3021#1{{}}\mathdef3021{result type} & \href{../text/types.html#text-resulttype}{\mathtt{resulttype}} &::=& (t{:}\href{../text/types.html#text-functype}{\mathtt{result}})^? &\Rightarrow& [t^?] \\ \end{array}\end{split}\]

Note

In future versions of WebAssembly, this scheme may be extended to support multiple results or more general result types.

Abbreviations¶

Multiple anonymous parameters or results may be combined into a single declaration:

\[\begin{split}\begin{array}{llclll} \def\mathdef3021#1{{}}\mathdef3021{parameter} & \def\mathdef3091#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3091{(}~~\def\mathdef3092#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3092{param}~~\href{../text/types.html#text-valtype}{\mathtt{valtype}}^\ast~~\def\mathdef3093#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3093{)} &\equiv& (\def\mathdef3094#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3094{(}~~\def\mathdef3095#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3095{param}~~\href{../text/types.html#text-valtype}{\mathtt{valtype}}~~\def\mathdef3096#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3096{)})^\ast \\ \def\mathdef3021#1{{}}\mathdef3021{result} & \def\mathdef3097#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3097{(}~~\def\mathdef3098#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3098{result}~~\href{../text/types.html#text-valtype}{\mathtt{valtype}}^\ast~~\def\mathdef3099#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3099{)} &\equiv& (\def\mathdef3100#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3100{(}~~\def\mathdef3101#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3101{result}~~\href{../text/types.html#text-valtype}{\mathtt{valtype}}~~\def\mathdef3102#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3102{)})^\ast \\ \end{array}\end{split}\]
